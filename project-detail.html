Here to Know About This Project

MeroDiscounts.com is a product of OCG Software Pvt Ltd. OC Group is a multinational company formed by a group of professionals having vivid experience and wide exposure in information technology, health care, real estate and management. This is Nepal’s first hyper-local online platform that enables customers and local merchants to discover and engage with each other
This application can be referred to as the “all-in-one solution" for all the needs, necessities, and miscellaneous requirements of every person living inside or outside Nepal. Whether you want to order any items to your doorstep, book a resort for family or friends outing, book flights or bus tickets, everything can be done with this single application.
