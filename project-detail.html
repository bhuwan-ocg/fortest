Merodiscounts

Mero Discounts is an all-in-one ecommerce store developed by the OCG Software Pvt. Ltd. This product is dedicated to providing a variety of best quality products and services to all the Nepalese living in Nepal and abroad.
Through Mero Discounts, we tend to support each and every local as well as high-end businesses to flourish through positive and advanced business consults, market analysis, and providing them with a platform to showcase their products and services. Bringing revolution in the way ecommerce runs in Nepal and making all the businesses as well as customers familiar with the online commerce is the major goal of Mero Discounts.

Features

Offers & Discounts

Get never-ending discounts and offers with every purchase from Mero Discounts. Any products or services that you choose comes with deals and discounts.

One-Stop Solution

Every product and services listed in one website and application to make it easy for the customers to get their hands on whatever they want.

Delivery Facility

We even provide home delivery services to the customers. Everything that you want right at your doorsteps.

Connection Establishment

Connecting every local vendor with their potential customers through unique strategies. Giving a dynamic platform for all the business owners to showcase and promote their services and products.

Fast Delivery
